About the Department

The Science Department at Deogiri College offers a diverse range of subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science. We aim to provide a solid foundation in scientific principles while nurturing analytical and critical thinking skills.

Courses Offered

  • B.Sc. in Biotechnology
  • M.Sc. in Physics
  • M.Sc. in Chemistry
  • Ph.D. in Life Sciences

Labs & Facilities

Our department features modern laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, along with a state-of-the-art computer lab for simulations and research work.
